Welcome! I am an assistant professor of statistics at Lawrence University. I enjoy teaching and working collaboratively to find meaning in complex data. My research interests include statistical machine learning, data mining, and statistics education. I earned my Ph.D. in statistics from Iowa State University in 2018. Prior to that, I earned M.S. and B.A. degrees in mathematics from Miami University and The College of Wooster , respectively. I enjoyed teaching high school mathematics in Northeastern Ohio from 2007-2011.

Please visit my teaching and research pages or see my CV to learn more. Check out my hobbies page to find out what else I enjoy.
