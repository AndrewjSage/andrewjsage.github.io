I earned my Ph.D. in statistics from Iowa State University in Spring 2018. I am excited to join the faculty of Lawrence University as assistant professor of statistics in Fall 2018.

Working with students invigorates me and I and look forward to engaging closely with undergraduates in my teaching and research.

My research interests include statistical machine learning, data mining, and statistics education. As a member of an interdisciplinary team, I use predictive analytics to improve student experiences and retention in STEM majors at Iowa State.

I grew up in Ohio, where I earned my B.A. and M.S. degrees in mathematics from The College of Wooster and Miami University, respectively.
