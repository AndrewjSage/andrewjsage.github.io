I enjoy teaching statistics and I look forward to working with closely with undergraduate students through teaching and mentoring undergraduate research at a liberal arts college.

I grew up in Ohio, where I earned my B.A. and M.S. degrees in mathematics from The College of Wooster and Miami University, respectively.
